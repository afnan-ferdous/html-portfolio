The Best Movies According to Afnan Ferdous

My top 3 movies of all-time

3 Idiots

An Indian motivational film produced by Mr. Rajkumar Hirani where the message of the film was learning to acquire knowledge not for success. If one acquires knowledge, he can be successful in any way.

Aynabaji

A Bangladeshi movie starring Chanchal Chowdhury. He was sentenced to death by impersonating a powerful political figure and escaped before the execution by hypnotizing the jailer.

Surongo

Afran Nisho acted in Bangladeshi film first time. It is a crime thriller movie based on the true story of an underground tunnel to steal money from a bank.
